Privacy Policy for DIGI7/h1>

This Privacy Policy explains how DIGI7 TOKEN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "your") when you use our blockchain platform and related services.

Introduction

At DIGI7, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and secure manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data while you use our platform, including our website and associated services. By accessing or using DIGI7 services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect two types of information

Personal Information

This includes data that identifies you as an individual. When you engage with the DIGI7 platform, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Government-Issued Identification (for KYC purposes)
  • Proof of Address
  • Transaction History and Blockchain Addresses

Non-Personal Information

This includes data that does not identify you as an individual but may include:

Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

As part of our AML/CFT compliance efforts, we require all users to comply with our Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures. This process ensures that we can identify and verify the identities of individuals and entities engaging with the DIGI7 platform.

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Cookies and usage data

Prohibited Activities

In the face of global environmental challenges, DIGIN Foundation is unwavering in its commitment to sustainability. The foundation supports eco-friendly projects, advocates for blockchain-based solutions to environmental issues, and funds initiatives that promote a harmonious balance between technological advancement and ecological responsibility.

How We Use Your Information

DIGI7 uses your personal and non-personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account Verification: To verify your identity and ensure compliance with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations..
  • Transaction Processing: To process your transactions, such as token transfers, staking, and liquidity mining activities.
  • Customer Support: To provide assistance in case of issues, respond to inquiries, and manage your account.
  • Improvement of Services: To enhance our platform, troubleshoot problems, and improve user experience.
  • Marketing Communications: To send updates, news, and promotional information (you can opt-out at any time).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We encrypt sensitive information such as your KYC data.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular audits to ensure our security protocols are up to date.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist in running our platform, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and KYC service providers. These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to law enforcement or regulatory authorities if required by law or to comply with a legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DIGI7 uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. Cookies help us:

  • Analyze how users interact with our website and services.
  • Provide personalized content and ads.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.

You can manage cookie settings in your browser. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the platform.

Your Rights and Control Over Your Data

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data held by DIGI7.
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal obligations.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent for processing your personal data at any time, without affecting the legality of processing based on consent before withdrawal.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can unsubscribe from marketing communications by following the instructions in the emails or by contacting us directly.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided in the Contact Information section below.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Once the information is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted.

International Transfers

If you are located outside of the jurisdiction where DIGI7 operates, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries, which may have different data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to other countries for processing.

Children’s Privacy

DIGI7 does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

DIGI7 reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
